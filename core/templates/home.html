Our Story

Building a Nigerian Business Empire.

Calawazobia Nigeria Limited is a fast-growing diversified group operating across four vital sectors. Born in Uyo and expanding into Abuja, we exist to deliver world-class products and experiences that uplift communities and redefine what Nigerian enterprise can be.

From the warm aroma of fresh bread in Uyo, to the elegance of our Abuja lounge, to the productivity of our farm and the comfort of our hotel — we are stitched together by one promise: excellence in everything we do.
