Our Story

A modern farm built on discipline & care.

Calawazobia Integrated Farm is a premium poultry operation based in Uyo, Akwa Ibom State. We raise our birds in clean, controlled environments with a strict feeding schedule — because the quality of an egg starts long before it reaches a tray.

Every bird is monitored. Every tray is inspected. Every supply run is consistent. That's how we've earned the trust of households, restaurants and resellers across the South-South.
